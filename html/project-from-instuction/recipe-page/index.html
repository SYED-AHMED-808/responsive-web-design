Omlet

this is recipe for omlet

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Onions
  • vegetable oil
  • regulare spices

Instructions

  1. cut the onions into small pieces
  2. add some chilli powder, salt, turmeric powder and ginger-garlic paste
  3. mix them with onions finely
  4. add a small amount of water droplet
  5. mix it more now and add a egg into the the mixture and mix more
  6. then put a pan on the stove and add oil
  7. let the oil get heated
  8. after the oil get heated pour egg paste into pan
  9. let it cook for 20sec
  10. flip that cooked egg back and let it cooked for 20sec
